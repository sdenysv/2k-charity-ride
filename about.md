Hello! I'm Denys, and this is my story "how I spent my summer vacation 2023".

I took a vacation from work and rode my bike from Maastricht, Netherlands to Andermatt, Switzerland and back. Why? To raise money for kids, but also to have advanture, see new places, and challenge myself. 

I rode 2,000 km in 18 days, and raised €2,000. I met amazing people, had injuries, and every second day I was asking myself "Why am I doing this?". But I made it, we made it, and I'm proud of us. 

It wouldn't be possible without the support of my family, friends, and all the donors. Thank you!
